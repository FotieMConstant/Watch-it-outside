Description

Boston Public Garden this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight.

Watch it outside Boston edition is a special event, an outdoor film festival in Boston Public Garden, a park in Boston from August 5th to 8th, 6pm to midnight. The event appeals to audiences of all ages, consequently everyone has his own piece of film to go through. At this event you will watch all kinds of films dating from 90s to those of today!
