About - Watch it outside Boston edition

Watch it outside Boston edition is a special event, an outdoor film festival in Boston Public Garden, a park in Boston from August 5th to 8th, 6pm to midnight. The event appeals to audiences of all ages, consequently everyone has his own piece of film to go through. At this event you will watch all kinds of films dating from 90s to those of today! if you are someone who is often stressed out with work and never find some time to take a break. Then this, for sure is the show for you!

Watch it outside Boston edition is Free! Just do your Reservation online here on our website to guarantee your seat. hope to see you soon!
